Cooked Ground Beef

Description

Ready for a more flavorful addition to your recipes? Learn how to cook ground beef the right way with tasteofhome.com's step-by-step guide.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Salt and pepper, to season the beef once cooked

Steps

  1. Heat your skillet

  2. Break up and add the beef

  3. Allow the beef to cook

  4. Break up and move beef around

  5. Drain the grease
